Vodka Martini Cocktail

Shaken, not stirred.

Ingredients

  • Vodka
  • Dry Vermouth for a floral and tart flavour
  • Olives, either Castelvetrano or Spanish

Directions

  1. Combine the vodka and dry vermouth in a mixing glass with ice. Stir until chilled, then strain into a chilled martini glass.
  2. Top the cocktail with as many olives as you like (threaded onto a toothpick).
